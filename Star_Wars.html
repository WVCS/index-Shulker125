Movie Summaries (Spoilers)

The Phantom Menace

The Phantom Menace is about Anakin Skywalker's origin story. The movie also talks about the growing conflict between the Trade Federation and the Rupublic. When the Trade Federation cuts off trade from the planet Naboo and establishes a blockade around it, Jedi knights Obi Wan Kenobi and Qui Gon Jin are tasked to end the conflict.

Attack of the Clones

Attack of the Clones is about the start of the Clone Wars which is the Republic against the Seperatist Alliance. In this movie, Obi Wan Kenobi travels to the planet Kamino to find out who was trying to assasinate Padme Amidala when he learns that the Kaminoans were producing a clone army for the Republic. The Rupublic uses these clones in their endeavor against the Seperatist Alliance.

Revenge of the Sith

Revenge of the Sith is about the end of the Clone Wars and the transition of the Galactic Republic to the Galactic Empire. When Anakin Skywalker sees his wife, Padme, die from childbirth in a dream, he sets off to try and prevent that from happening. In the end he turns to the dark side because of Chancellor Palpatine who assures Anakin that he can save Padme.

A New Hope

A New Hope is about Luke Skywalker's origin story and how he joins the Rebels to overthrow the Empire. Starting on the sandy planet of Tatooine, Luke Skywalker learns about the Jedi from Obi Wan Kenobi and begins his training to become a Jedi knight. He and the Rebels then plan an assault on the Empire's Death Star which threatens destruction to the galaxy.

The Empire Strikes Back

The Empire Strikes Back is about the start of Luke's training under Master Yoda to become a Jedi and the Empire's attempts to snuff out the Rebels. After the Empire's assault on the Rebel base on Hoth, Luke Skywalker travels to Dagobah where he learns the essentials in becoming a Jedi. Afterwards, when he sees his friends in trouble on Bespin, he leaves Yoda and promises to complete his training after.

The Return of the Jedi

The Return of the Jedi is about the Rebels' fight to destroy the second Death Star. Luke, Leia, Chewbacca, and Lando fight Jabba the Hutt in order to save Han Solo. After, Luke returns to Dagobah to finish his training with Yoda. When the Rebels learn of the threat of the second Death Star, they devise a plan to take it out by disabling it's shields from the planet Endor.

The Force Awakens

The Force Awakens is about a scavenger named Rey who joins the Resistance. Rey and Finn, a former stormtrooper, have to bring a droid named BB-8 to the Resistance. In their travels, the meet Han Solo who helps them in their endeavours. When they arrive at the Resistance base, Rey, Finn, Han, and Chewbacca must now face a new threat to the galaxy: Starkiller Base.

The Last Jedi

The Last Jedi is about the Resistance's struggle to escape the First Order after being chased out of their base. With low fuel in their capital ship, the Resistance sends a team to try and disable the First Order's tracking device in order to escape. Meanwile, Rey finds Luke Skywalker and learns what it takes to become a Jedi.

The Rise of Skywalker

The Rise of Skywalker is about the return of Palpatine and the Sith Fleet. In order to find the Sith Fleet, Rey, Finn, and Poe must find a Sith Wayfinder. Their mission crosses paths with the First Order countless of times. When the Resistance finds the planet of Exegol, they find that the fleet is almost ready to be deployed. In the end, Rey must battle Palpatine to decide the fate of the galaxy.
